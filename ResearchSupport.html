Research Support

Research Support Training

The Leisure Library offers Research Support Training and Workshops for students, researchers, academicians, and the public. This includes free workshops, paid workshops for the public or specific modules, and on-demand training. Our aim is to enhance research skills and knowledge dissemination.

Community Forum

Connect with fellow researchers in our community forum. Share insights, seek advice, and engage in discussions to foster collaboration and knowledge exchange.

Tips on How to Support Predatory Journals

Navigate the complex landscape of scholarly publishing. Access valuable tips on identifying and avoiding predatory journals, ensuring the credibility and impact of your research.

Journal Citation Report

Explore the impact of journals in your field using our Journal Citation Report. Access data on citation metrics, journal rankings, and make informed decisions on where to publish your research.

Publishing Guide

Navigate the publishing process seamlessly with our Publishing Guide. From manuscript preparation to submission, peer review, and post-publication, we provide step-by-step guidance

Research Tools

Empower your research journey with our suite of research tools. From reference management to data analysis, we offer a range of tools to streamline your workflow.
