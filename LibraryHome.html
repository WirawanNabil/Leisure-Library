Latest News!

Gotong Royong Perdana Programme

10 December 2023 - Gotong Royong Perdana Programme was organized by Leisure Library Management. This programme was held from 8.00 am until 4.30 pm. Well done and congratulations to all Leisure Library residents for implementing this Gotong Royong Perdana Programme.

Leisure Library Grand Opening

25 November 2023 - The grand opening of Leisure Library was held in order to notify the community that their awaited nearest library from their home is finally here. This grand opening was organized by Leisure Library Management and collaboration with the National Library Association (NLA).
